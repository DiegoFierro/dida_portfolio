Our Instruments

Authentic Berimbaus

Handcrafted from high-quality wood and natural gourds, producing the soul-stirring sound essential to Capoeira.

Powerful Atabaques

Durable drums with rich, resonant tones, perfect for setting the rhythm and energy of the Roda.

Vibrant Pandeiros

Lightweight and responsive tambourines, adding intricate percussive layers to your music.

Rhythmic Caxixis

Small, hand-woven rattles that provide a crisp, percussive sound, often played with the Berimbau.

Melodic Agogôs

A double or triple bell instrument that adds a distinct metallic tone and melodic rhythm to the Capoeira orchestra.

Scratching Reco-Recos

A percussive instrument made from a hollow wooden or bamboo body with grooves, played by scraping a stick across them.
